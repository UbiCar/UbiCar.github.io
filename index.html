Short Bio

My name is Henry(Chuan-Heng), Lin. I hold B.S.E and M.S. degree from Department of Civil Engineering at National Taiwan University with research interests in ubiquitous computing and distributed decision support system advised by Albert Y. Chen.

Previously, I was a data engineer in Xynosys.Inc. My role involved in mining customer behavior in enterprise database, analyze the social interaction with text context and visualize the report for customer. It is really fun to dig into the whole pipeline.

Now, I serve as an alternative civilian service in Exhibition Center Construction Task Force at Bureau of Foreign Trade, where allowed me to understand how government build up important constructions that benefit to citizens.

By the way, I am also a basketball player and a photographer. I play for a company team in an amateur basketball league every weekends and sometimes I use my camera to see the world from different angle.

Thank you for visiting my website.
